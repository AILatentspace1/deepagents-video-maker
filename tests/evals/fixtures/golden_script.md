## Audio Design

**Music:**
- **Scene 1-3 (The Hook):** Minimal electronic synthesizer. Arpeggiators that evoke technology and data. "Neon Future" vibe.
- **Scene 4 (No Compromises):** Shift to a warmer, analog pad sound. More human, reflective.
- **Scene 5 (The New Paradigm):** Introduce a rhythmic bass pulse. Driving, confident.
- **Scene 6 (Multi-Agent):** Back to digital, slightly chaotic/glitchy textures to represent the "noise" of multiple agents.
- **Scene 7 (Death of Icon):** Deep, sub-bass drones. Serious, cinematic, almost ominous but not scary.
- **Scene 8 (Infinite Loop):** Uplifting, major key progression. Combines the digital synth with a piano melody to symbolize human + AI harmony.

**Sound Effects (SFX):**
- **Typing:** Mechanical keyboard sounds (very ASMR, satisfying) synced with the visuals of prompts.
- **UI Sounds:** "Success" chimes when an app is generated. Soft clicks for navigation.
- **Ambience:** A very subtle room tone or "server hum" underneath the narration to keep it grounded.
- **Transitions:** Digital "whooshes" or data-stream sounds to cut between scenes.
